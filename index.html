Description

Cows are a relatively large form animal in minecraft, with adults being 1.4 blocks tall and 0.9 blocks wide. All cows are mainly brown with grey patches. Also all cows have two horns on their head and udders underneath their stomach.

For more information, check out the Minecraft Wiki page for cows: Minecraft Cow Wiki
